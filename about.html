Our Story

Founded in 2010, AutoCare began as a small neighborhood garage with just two mechanics. Today, we've grown into one of the most trusted auto service centers in the region, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Our founder, Michael Johnson, started AutoCare with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable car repairs at fair prices. That commitment remains at the heart of everything we do.
