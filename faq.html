Service Process

Service duration depends on the type of service needed. A general service typically takes 2-3 hours, while more complex repairs may take a full day or longer. When you book your appointment, we'll provide an estimated completion time based on your specific needs.

We recommend booking an appointment to ensure we can serve you promptly. While we do accept walk-ins, priority is given to customers with appointments. Emergency services are available 24/7 for roadside assistance.

Please bring your vehicle registration, insurance information, and any service records you may have. If your vehicle is under warranty, bring your warranty information as well. Remove personal belongings from your vehicle before service.

Pricing & Payments

We provide estimates because the exact cost of repairs can't be determined until we inspect your vehicle. Some issues may be more or less extensive than initially apparent. We'll always contact you for approval before performing any work beyond the original estimate.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, cash, and checks. We also offer financing options for major repairs through our partners.

Yes! We offer a 10% discount for seniors, military personnel, and first responders. We also have seasonal promotions and package deals. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about current offers.

Warranties

All our services come with a minimum 3-month warranty. Most parts we install have manufacturer warranties ranging from 1 year to lifetime, depending on the part. We'll provide full warranty details with your service report.

Yes, if a warranted repair fails, we'll cover towing to our facility within 25 miles. For longer distances, we'll reimburse up to $100 of towing charges upon verification.

Policies

We request at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations. There's no charge for cancellations made with sufficient notice. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a $25 fee, except in emergency situations.

Absolutely! You can reschedule your appointment anytime through our online portal or by calling us. We recommend giving us as much notice as possible to accommodate your preferred time.

We'll always contact you for approval before performing any additional work. Our technicians will explain the needed repairs, provide an updated estimate, and get your authorization before proceeding.
